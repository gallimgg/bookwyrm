HOW TO PLAY

GAME OVERVIEW

Book Wyrm is a literary guessing game where you face off against a mystical dragon who guards ancient texts. Your mission is to identify books from their opening passages, proving your worth as a keeper of literary knowledge.

GAMEPLAY RULES

  • You'll be presented with a passage from a famous book
  • Your task is to identify both the book's title and its author
  • Use your arrows wisely - each one reveals more of the text or provides multiple choice options
  • The fewer arrows you use, the more impressive your victory over the Book Wyrm
  • You can play in Daily Challenge mode for a new curated passage each day
  • Or choose Free Play mode to test your knowledge across our entire library

SCORING

  • Each arrow used adds to your count - try to use as few as possible
  • A perfect victory is achieved by guessing correctly without using any arrows
  • Both the book title and author must be correct to win
